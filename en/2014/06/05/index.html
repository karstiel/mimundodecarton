Last shoppings: clothes and shoes (2014 Spring)

During these last months, I have bought a lot of things, in Internet and in my city.

I will begin with an anorak which I have searched for long time. I wanted that it was waterproof, warm and cozy and following my style. I have found it in an outlet of Desigual which had the three requirements. It cost less than 90€ and I am happy with it but just now hot has been arrived and I can’t use it. The model is “Midnight Jacket”, black color.

Midnight Jacket Desigual

Also I have bought a military jacket in a small shop of my city (Palma de Mallorca) and it cost 20€. I like how it is adapted to me and it is so comfortable, because the material is elastic. It has adornment buttons but it is closed with hook and eyes. We had to put more hook and eyes which it had (and sew again the originals) because it didn’t close correctly.

Elastic military jacket

I have bought another military jacket, but in leather, by eBay. This seller had a lot of victorian things and I was bid for some of them, but I won only the jacket and a black long skirt.

My favourite bondage trouser brand was Tripp, but I found another ones which I liked so much and also they had good prize. They are from Poizen Industries brand and the shop is Kinkyangel. Also I have bought two black short dresses, one of them which I wanted it since one year ago (I have it!). I have put a photo (the first) in my post about daily Summer dresses.
